Why Should I groom my Dog?

Grooming is one of your dogs basic needs.Regular grooming of your dog promotes healthy skin and coat helping to keep at bay or even eliminate parasites. Grooming is an important part of dog care. All Breeds require daily grooming, how much depends on the breed, age, or health of the pet. Regular grooming helps to ensure the dog is healthy and comfortable.
